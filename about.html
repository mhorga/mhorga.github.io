I am one of the very first Udacity iOS Code Reviewers, a Community Teaching Assistant at Coursera, and an independent iOS developer.

You can read more about my iOS activity on this blog, read my answers on StackOverflow, and see my Github contributions here and here. I also published Space Explorer to the Apple app store, the first ever iOS app to show the previously unreleased NASA images from all the Apollo missions.

I am open to discuss about interesting opportunities in iOS development. You can reach out to me on LinkedIn or Twitter. You can also use the Contact form on this web site.
