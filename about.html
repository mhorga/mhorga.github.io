I am one of the very first Udacity iOS Code Reviewers, a Community Teaching Assistant at Coursera, and an independent iOS developer.

You can read more about my current iOS activity on this blog, and see some of the iOS projects I'm working on Github. I also published Space Explorer to the Apple app store, the first ever iOS app to show the previously unreleased NASA images from all the Apollo missions.

I am open to discuss about interesting opportunities in iOS development. You can reach out to me on LinkedIn, Twitter or StackOverflow. You can also use the Contact form on this web site.
